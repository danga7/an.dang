An Dang is a Vietnamese multidisciplinary designer based in Helsinki, Finland.

She enjoys telling stories and solving problems with creative visuals in different forms.

After completing her Bachelor of Arts at XAMK University of Applied Sciences in Kouvola, Finland, An continued her academic journey in New Media studies at Aalto University School of Arts, Design and Architecture in Helsinki, Finland.

Currently, she is working to wrap up her Master's of Arts degree, as well as working part-time as a Data Specialist and looking for a creative job position.

Her practices include UI/ Visual Design, Data Visualization, Illustration and other mixed-media arts.
